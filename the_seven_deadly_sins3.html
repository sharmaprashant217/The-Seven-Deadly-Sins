{{The Seven Deadly Sins "Imperial Wrath Of The Gods"}}

With Camelot now the stronghold of the Ten Commandments, the Seven Deadly Sins assemble once more to liberate all of Britannia from the Demon Clan.

Season 3 Episodes List


1.The Light That Sweeps Away the Darkness:-

Meliodas and Ban intercept a caravan transporting prisoners to the Ten Commandments, but demons still threaten villages in other parts of Britannia.


2.Memories of the Holy War:-

King and Diane take refuge in the Fairy King's Forest to escape the Ten Commandments. But Gloxinia and Drole find them after a peaceful interlude.


3. Let There Be Light:-

Looking like Gloxinia and Drole, King and Diane are transported back to Britannia 3,000 years ago. There they meet Meliodas, who's fighting for Stigma.


4.The Ten Commandments vs. The Archangels:-

Led by Ludociel, the Four Archangels overwhelm the Ten Commandments. With no other choice, Derieri and Monspeet resort to extreme measures.


5.Emotional Maelstrom:-

Elizabeth attempts to save Derieri and Monspeet from their Indura transformation. Defying Ludociel, Sariel and Tarmiel go to her aid.


6.We Call That Love:-

The original Gowther comes to Diane and King with a request. King learns of a human revolt against Stigma and rushes to Gerheade's aid.


7.Deadly Sins,Unite!!:-

King regains consciousness in the present, but Diane is still stuck in the past facing Zeldris. Drole watches calmly, but King is frantic with worry.


8.The Doll Seeks Love:-

While King and the others search for him, the events that led Gowther to suppress his memories come to light. Then he meets Nadja.


9.The Cursed Lovers:-

Diane tells Elizabeth that she met someone who looked just like her in the past. Meliodas denies knowing anything about it when Elizabeth asks.


10.The Life We Live:-

On their way to liberate Camelot, Meliodas tells his comrades about the curse that two deities put upon him and Elizabeth 3,000 years ago.


11.The Hateful Cannot Rest:-

The party heads to the city of Coland to dispel the distortion protecting Camelot. When Meliodas is trapped in darkness, they must fight without him.


12. Love is a Maidenâ€™s Power:-

Transformed into a giant snake, Melascula attacks Ban and the others. After Meliodas escapes the darkness, he unleashes his fearsome Assault Mode.


13.The Almighty vs. the Greatest Evil:-

Meliodas regains the strength he had when he led the Ten Commandments. Escanor, whose power peaks at the stroke of noon, struggles against him.


14.A New Threat:-

The Demon King orders Zeldris to capture Meliodas and kill the Deadly Sins. But when he calls upon the Ten Commandments, no one answers.


15.To Our Captain:-

Chandler, Meliodas' former mentor, aids Zeldris. He infiltrates the Boar Hat to capture Meliodas, but the Deadly Sins stand in his way.


16.The Seven Deadly Sins End:-

King prepares to face Chandler, who's returned in his demonic form. Gloxinia and Drole appear, risking their lives so the Deadly Sins can escape.


17.Our Choices:-

Meliodas disbands the Deadly Sins and departs with Elizabeth. But his companions aren't going to let him go without a fight, and Hawk holds the key.


18.March of the Saints:-

Merlin faces Zeldris, whom Meliodas has ordered to gather the Commandments. Margaret, Hendrickson and Dreyfus continue their search for Gilthunder.


19.The Holy War Pact:-

Having taken over Margaret's body, Ludociel appears in Liones and declares a holy war against the Demon Clan. Chandler searches for Elizabeth.


20.Child of Hope:-

In Camelot, Arthur searches for the Holy Sword Excalibur alone. Ludociel warns Escanor to give up his Grace of Sunshine, on pain of death.


21. Beginning of the Holy War:-

Inside Camelot, Arthur makes his last stand against Meliodas and the others. Merlin comes to his rescue, but not before she falls under Cusack's spell.


22.War-torn Britannia:-

The new Holy War commences as the allied forces of Stigma face off against the Demon Clan. Derieri decides to surrender her Commandment to Zeldris.


23.The One Twisted by Darkness:-

Sensing Elizabeth's magic, Derieri appears before the Stigma army. Estarossa follows her, but Sariel and Tarmiel have other plans for him.


24. Rampaging Love:-

Estarossa professes his love for Elizabeth. When he loses control after absorbing three of the Commandments, Stigma's army faces him in a final battle.
